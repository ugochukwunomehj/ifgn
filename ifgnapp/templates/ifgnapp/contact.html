Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 business hours. For urgent matters related to forensic services, please call our main office directly at +2349047427213.

Do I need to make an appointment to visit your campus?

Yes, we recommend scheduling an appointment for campus visits to ensure that appropriate staff members are available to assist you. You can schedule a visit by calling our main office or using the contact form on this page.

How do I submit documents for examination?

Document submission procedures depend on the nature of the case. Please contact our Forensic Services department at services@ifgn.ng for specific instructions. We have strict protocols for maintaining chain of custody and document integrity.

Can I request a consultation before enrolling in a course?

Yes, we offer pre-enrollment consultations to help prospective students determine the most appropriate course for their professional goals. Contact our Admissions department at admissions@ifgn.ng to schedule a consultation.
