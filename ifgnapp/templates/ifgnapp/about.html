Our Journey

The IFGN Story

2010

Foundation

IFGN was established by a group of forensic experts and legal professionals to address the growing need for qualified document examiners in Nigeria's legal and financial sectors.

2020

Certified. Accredited. Globally Recognized

IFGN is a legally recognized professional body and institute for higher learning in Nigeria, incorporated under CAMA 2020. It is officially authorized by the Federal Ministries of Education and Justice, ensuring legal integrity, academic credibility, and professional standards.

2016

Research Center Established

Opened our dedicated research center with state-of-the-art equipment for advanced document analysis and research in forensic graphology.

2019

A Global Network. A Local Impact

Through strategic global partnerships, IFGN provides access to best practices, research collaborations, faculty exchanges, and career certification opportunities.

2022

A Community of Thinkers, Truth-Seekers & Professionals

Joining IFGN means becoming part of a mission-driven community dedicated to advancing forensic handwriting analysis through high-impact training and collaboration.

Today

Leading the Field

Today, IFGN stands as the premier institution for forensic graphology in West Africa, with over 500 graduates making significant contributions to the field across the continent.

State-of-the-Art Facilities

Document Analysis Laboratory

Our main laboratory is equipped with advanced microscopes, spectral analysis tools, and comparison equipment for detailed examination of questioned documents.

  • Video Spectral Comparators
  • Digital Microscopy Stations
  • UV/IR Imaging Systems

Digital Forensics Laboratory

Our digital lab features the latest technology for examining electronic documents, digital signatures, and performing computer forensic analysis.

  • Digital Signature Analysis Software
  • Electronic Document Authentication Tools
